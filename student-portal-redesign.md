# Student Portal Redesign Plan

🤖 **Applying knowledge of `@frontend-specialist`...**

## 🎯 The Problem
The current portal uses a dark gradient hero contrasting with a glassmorphism (semi-transparent) white card. While aesthetic, it creates inherent contrast issues (WCAG fail) when displaying dense tabular data like student records. It falls into the "Glass Trap" (a common design cliché).

## 🧠 Deep Design Thinking (Anti-Safe Harbor)
To fix readability while keeping the site feeling premium and modern, we must abandon the "Safe Split" and "Glassmorphism" clichés. We need a design that screams **"Official Academic Record"** rather than "Generic SaaS Dashboard".

### 🎨 DESIGN COMMITMENT: "Swiss Academic Ledger"

- **Topological Choice**: We are moving to a sharp, high-contrast, edge-to-edge structured layout. Think of a premium printed academic transcript or a Swiss-style structured ledger.
- **Risk Factor**: No soft rounded corners (`rounded-3xl`) or floating blurry shadows (`backdrop-blur`). We will use stark 1px/2px solid borders, bold geometric typography, and a strict column grid to make the data 100% legible, authoritative, and fast to read.
- **Readability Conflict**: Solved by extreme contrast. Deep ink-black text (`#0a0a0a`) on an off-white/physical "paper" background (`#F4F4F0`).
- **Cliché Liquidation**: Killing glassmorphism and aurora glows entirely.
