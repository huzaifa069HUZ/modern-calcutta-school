Surah Al-Alaq (96:1)

The first revelation of the Quran emphasizes the importance of knowledge and learning. At Modern Calcutta School, we build our foundation on this divine commandment, nurturing minds and souls through Islamic education.

Iman Faith

The unshakable belief in the Oneness of Allah and His Messenger, forming the bedrock of existence.

"Faith is a knowledge in the heart, a voicing with the tongue, and an activity with the limbs."

01

Ilm Knowledge

Pursuing sacred and worldly knowledge as a lifelong act of worship and illumination.

"Seek knowledge from the cradle to the grave."

02

Akhlaq Character

Embodying the noble character traits of the Prophet (PBUH) in every interaction.

"The best amongst you are those who have the best manners and character."

03

Adab Etiquette

Refining one's manners with elders, teachers, peers, and the environment.

"Adab (etiquette) is the door to the acceptance of good deeds."

04

Khidmah Service

Serving humanity as a manifestation of one's love for the Creator. We teach that the leader of a people is their servant.

"The most beloved of people to Allah are those who are most beneficial to people."
