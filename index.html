At Modern Calcutta School, we dissolve the boundaries between religious values and practical education. We believe that Character (Adab) is the foundation of Competence (Ilm).

Our curriculum integrates the timeless wisdom of the Quran with cutting-edge academic standards. Students don't just learn to excel in the workforce; they learn to serve humanity with compassion, integrity, and a God-conscious heart.

Islamic Values

Rooted in Iman, integrating Quranic studies & ethics for a strong moral compass.

Modern Education

Global standards with STEM, robotics, and critical thinking for future leaders.

Traditional Wisdom

Reviving Adab (etiquette) and heritage to foster identity and pride.

Holistic Care

Nurturing the heart, soul, and mind in a supportive environment.
