Leadership

Hafiz Shanauddin

Director & Founder

"Education is not just about filling minds with knowledge, but about illuminating hearts with wisdom and compassion. At Modern Calcutta School, we strive to nurture both the intellect and the soul."

Mohammad Noorshanuddin, known as Hafiz Shanuddin, is a Hafiz-e-Quran, Graduate, and Special Educator with over 10 years of teaching experience. He is also a poet, writer, and preacher of comparative religion, dedicated to promoting knowledge, harmony, and value-based education. As the Founder of Shan and Nikhat Educational & Welfare Trust, he is committed to uplifting educational standards and fostering holistic development. He strongly believes in integrating Islamic values with CBSE-based modern education to nurture students who are academically competent and morally grounded. Under his leadership, the institution follows structured learning methodologies, including Modern Teaching Methods (MTM), effective Teaching Learning Materials (TLM), and progressive stages of learning to ensure conceptual clarity and lifelong understanding.
